How to Play Wordle - Complete Guide

Step 1: Guess the Word

Type any 5-letter word to start. You have 6 attempts to guess the correct word.

Step 2: Understand the Colors

Green = Correct letter in correct position

Yellow = Correct letter in wrong position

Gray = Letter not in the word

Step 3: Use Logic

Each guess gives you information. Use it to narrow down the possible words!

Wordle Tips for Beginners

  • Start with words containing common letters (E, A, R, O, T, L)
  • Don't repeat letters too early
  • Pay attention to letter positions
