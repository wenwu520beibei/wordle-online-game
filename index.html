How to Play Wordle

Wordle is a daily word guessing game that has taken the world by storm. The goal is simple: guess a 5-letter word in just 6 attempts.

How to Play

  • Type any 5-letter word to start playing
  • Green means the letter is correct and in the right position
  • Yellow means the letter is in the word but wrong position
  • Gray means the letter is not in the word
  • You have 6 attempts to guess the word

Wordle Tips & Strategies

Start with words that contain common letters like E, A, R, O, T, L, I, N, S. Avoid repeating letters in your early guesses unless you have no other options.

Why Wordle is So Popular

Wordle has become a viral sensation because it's simple, free, and only takes a few minutes to play. Plus, you can share your results without spoiling the answer for others!
