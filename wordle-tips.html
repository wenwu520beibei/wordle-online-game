🎯 Wordle Tips & Strategies

1. Start with Common Letters

Use words with common letters like E, A, R, O, T, L, I, N, S

2. Don't Repeat Letters Too Early

Save repeated letters for later guesses when you know the word has them.

3. Use Process of Elimination

Gray letters eliminate possibilities. Focus on finding new letters.

4. Pay Attention to Position

Yellow means right letter, wrong spot. Green means right letter, right spot.

5. Save Your Vowels

Most words have at least one vowel. Find them early!
